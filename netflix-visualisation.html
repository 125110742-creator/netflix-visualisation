Netflix Content Strategy · 2013 – 2021

The Gap Is Closing

Netflix began as a film platform. From 2016, TV show additions grew steadily year on year — rising from 18% of catalogue additions in 2014 to nearly half by 2021. Movies still lead, but the margin has narrowed sharply.
